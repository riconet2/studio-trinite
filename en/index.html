Around You

La Trinité-sur-Mer is a vibrant and authentic harbour town, at the heart of the bay of Quiberon.

🚶On foot from the studio
  • The harbour, beaches and the coastal path (GR34)
  • Bakeries, shops and restaurants
  • The salt marshes of La Trinité
💙Our favourite spot

We particularly love Kervilen beach — perfect for a stroll or a relaxing moment by the sea.

🛶Water sports & sea activities
  • Kayak & paddle: Run'O, Varec'h Kayak, Kerners Kayak & Paddle
  • Electric boats (no licence needed): E.SEA
  • Guided boat tours: Vedettes Angels
  • Lunch/dinner cruises: Navix, Le Passeur des Îles
  • Ferry to Belle-Île island: regular service or sailing (Iliens)
🚴Cycling
  • Le Vélo Trinitain (on site), VéloCarnac, L'Original Location (Erdeven)
  • Recommended route: La Trinité → Locmariaquer (~34 km, bike paths and country lanes)
👕Laundromats
  • ⭐ Le Salon Lavoir — our recommendation — 36 cours des Quais, open 7/7 from 7am to 9pm
  • Laverie Raz la Crêpe — Place Voulien (dryers may be unreliable)
🏖️Must-see sites nearby
  • Carnac standing stones (menhirs and dolmens)
  • Gavrinis (island & neolithic cairn)
  • Penthièvre isthmus & wild coast — sand yachting
  • Thalasso & sea spa in Carnac
  • Saint-Goustan harbour (Auray)
  • Walk along the Bono river (starting at St-Avoye chapel)
  • La Trinitaine biscuit factory
🏰Further afield — Vannes & Gulf of Morbihan
  • Vannes and its historic old town
  • Château de Suscinio · Poul Fétan (reconstructed Breton village)
  • Islands: Île aux Moines, Île d'Arz
  • Traditional boat sailing: Krog e Barz, Voiles du Golfe André & Yvette
  • Eco kayak tours: sentiersmarins.com
  • Helicopter & skydiving at Monterblanc
  • Vannes City Pass: discounts and transport included
🎶Lorient & the Interceltique Festival
  • Lorient, a dynamic port city 45 min from the studio
  • Festival Interceltique (early August): one of the world's largest Celtic festivals
  • Keroman fishing port (France's largest fishing harbour)
  • Cité de la Voile Éric Tabarly sailing museum
